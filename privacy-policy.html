Ermine Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-14

What Ermine stores

Ermine stores your session token, user ID, and selected API URL in browser cookies so you can stay signed in.

What Ermine does not store

Ermine does not store your personal chat data, profile content, or server data on Ermine repositories or Ermine-operated servers.

Service endpoints

Ermine communicates with official stoat.chat services (API, websocket, and CDN endpoints configured in the app).

Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact the Ermine maintainers through the project repository.
